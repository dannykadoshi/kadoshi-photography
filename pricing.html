Good to Know

Booking Deposit A 25% non-refundable deposit is required to secure your date. The balance is due 7 days before your session.
Travel Policy Travel within 60km of Co. Wexford is included in all session prices. Beyond that, a travel fee of €0.50/km applies. Ireland-wide available.
Image Delivery Portrait and pet sessions: delivered within 7–10 days. Weddings: 4–6 weeks. Corporate events: sneak peek within 24h, full gallery within 7 days.
Raw Files Raw files are not provided. All deliverables are professionally edited and colour-graded images in high-resolution formats suitable for print and digital use.
Corporate Invoicing VAT receipts provided for all corporate and business bookings. Quote requests welcome for multi-event or retainer arrangements.
Rescheduling Life happens. Sessions can be rescheduled once with 48 hours notice at no extra charge. Weddings follow a separate rescheduling policy discussed at booking.
