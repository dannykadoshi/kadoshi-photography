About

The Eye Behind
the Lens

I'm Danni Kadoshi — a photographer obsessed with the space between a blink and a memory. Whether I'm courtside at a championship match, deep in a mountain range at sunrise, documenting the tears and laughter of a wedding day, or chasing a golden dog through a field, my mission is always the same: capture what feels true.

Based in Co. Wexford, Ireland, my work spans sports, weddings, portraits, family, landscapes, street photography, pets, and events — because life doesn't fit in one box, and neither does my lens. Available across Ireland and for travel worldwide. Every session is approached with full commitment, technical precision, and a genuine love for storytelling.
