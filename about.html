My Story

Danni Kadoshi

I'm Danni Kadoshi — a photographer obsessed with the space between a blink and a memory. Whether I'm courtside at a championship match, deep in a mountain range at sunrise, documenting the tears and laughter of a wedding day, or chasing a golden dog through a field, my mission is always the same: capture what feels true.

Based in Co. Wexford, Ireland, my work spans sports, weddings, portraits, couples, family, landscapes, street photography, pets, and corporate events — because life doesn't fit in one box, and neither does my lens. Available across Ireland and for travel worldwide. Every session is approached with full commitment, technical precision, and a genuine love for storytelling.

I believe the best photographs aren't made — they're recognised. A split second of honesty, of vulnerability, of pure joy. My job is to be present enough, patient enough, and skilled enough to be there when it happens. Then get out of the way and let the moment breathe.
